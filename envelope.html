What are A4, A5, and DL Size Envelopes?

A4, A5, and DL size envelopes are standardized envelope sizes used for various types of business and personal correspondence. These envelopes are designed to accommodate standard A4 and A5 paper sizes and are ideal for sending letters, invoices, brochures, flyers, and other documents.
