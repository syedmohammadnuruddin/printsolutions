What Are Frosted Glass Stickers?

Frosted glass stickers are adhesive films designed to create the elegant appearance of frosted or etched glass on windows, glass doors, and other transparent surfaces. They offer the benefits of privacy, light diffusion, and visual appeal while maintaining the transparency and illumination of the glass.
